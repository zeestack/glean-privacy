Glean — Support

Glean is a private document scanner by TechFoundry. Scan documents into searchable PDFs, kept on your device and optionally saved to your own Google Drive.

Contact

Questions, feedback, or issues: zahid@tech-foundry.com. We aim to reply within 2 business days.

Common topics

  • Scanning: tap the Scan button, capture pages, then crop, rotate, filter, or “Clean up”.
  • Searchable PDFs: text is recognized on your device so PDFs are searchable; no internet needed.
  • Google Drive (optional): connect it in the Cloud tab to also save PDFs to a folder you choose. Scans are always kept on your device too.
  • Sign in: use Sign in with Apple or Continue with Google.
  • Delete your data: Account menu → “Delete account & data”.
